Why RxShield Matters?

For Patients

Medication errors are a leading cause of hospitalizations. RxShield empowers patients by:

  • Instant checks on prescription images.
  • Simple, jargon-free explanations of risks.
  • Offline access to local drug details (Prices, Side-effects).
  • Peace of Mind: Know your meds are safe together.

For Doctors

In a busy clinic, manual cross-checking is time-consuming. RxShield serves as a reliable second opinion:

  • Double Verification: AI analysis backed by clinical RxNav data.
  • Automated digitization of handwritten notes.
  • Quick history lookup for patient profiles.
  • Reduces liability by catching potential interactions early.

The Technology

Under the hood, RxShield combines multiple state-of-the-art systems:

  • Hybrid OCR: Cleans noisy image data for high precision.
  • Knowledge Graph: Visualizes relationships between drugs.
  • Generative AI: Understands context (Age, Weight) to tailor warnings.
